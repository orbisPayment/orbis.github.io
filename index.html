About Us

Orbis Payment Services, Inc. is an exceptionally dynamic credit card processing company. Orbis’ suite of products and services covers the most complete spectrum of payment options available today. Founded in 2001, Orbis is committed to its mission of providing merchants exceptional customer service and reliable products and technologies designed to help meet their business objectives. Orbis’ team understands the most essential card-service needs of each and every client: exceptional service, competitive pricing, superior products, and customized solutions. Whether a merchant has one location or a nationwide chain, Orbis has a solution to meet the business’ needs. The company works hard to achieve its high level of service and competitiveness by steadfastly adhering to the core values that have formed the essence and foundation of the company: honesty, integrity, and loyalty.

Orbis Payment Services was founded with merchants’ needs in mind. The company’s founders set out to build a payment services business dedicated to personal service and higher moral values. Orbis aims not to simply acquire new clients, but to provide them with the tools and support to manage and expand their businesses. The Orbis Payment Services team feels strongly about educating each merchant effectively on the complex world of credit card processing. By sharing its experience and expertise, Orbis is able to show merchants new ways of saving money, improving business practices, and adding value with new and innovative products and technologies. In an industry that regularly experiences high levels of turnover, Orbis does not. The company feels that the education it provides, its fair pricing practices, and the partnership it establishes with its merchants, leads to a high level of customer satisfaction. Orbis retains customers at rates unheard of in the merchant services industry.

The patient, friendly, and responsive customer support team, competitive pricing, customized solutions and innovative products set Orbis apart and ensures that Orbis Payment Services will be a merchant’s final stop when searching for a credit card processing company.

To have an Orbis sales professional provide you with a free analysis of your credit card processing rates and fees or to see how we can improve your level of service, please contact our Sales Department at (415) 883-8699.

Get Started

1. Consultation

Orbis will run an analysis of the merchant’s current processing statements, preferably from the previous three months. In addition to demonstrating current costs vs. proposed costs, Orbis’ detailed and thoughtful analysis can provide much more for perspective merchants:

  • Compare current processing fees to those proposed by Orbis.
  • Find any hidden or unnecessary fees being charged to the merchant.
  • Determine if the current merchant account was set up correctly.
  • Review the current pricing model and compare that with the merchant agreement to ensure that they align.
  • Analyze the merchant’s bank statements and compare them with the merchant’s processing statements to identify any reoccurring or duplicate charges that may have been processed without the merchants knowledge

Through this consultation, Orbis consistently finds ways to improve the security and financial efficiency for our perspective business partners. This consultation is a proven asset that sets Orbis apart from other merchant service providers, and is consistent with Orbis’ hands-on approach to business.

2. Complete Forms

If the decision is made to partner with Orbis, the merchant will need to complete three forms. Please contact us and we will provide them to you. These forms can be faxed to Orbis: (415) 883-8770

3. Schedule an Install

It takes three to five business days for applications to be processed. Once an application is approved, Orbis will contact the merchant to schedule an installation. Unlike other processors, Orbis will install the equipment, recycle or return old equipment to the previous processor, and fully train a merchant’s staff on how to use the new machine.

PCI Compliance

PCI Compliance greatly reduces the risk of payment card compromise, but what if security gaps are overlooked? These vulnerabilities can lead to a data breach, often resulting in customer loss, brand damage, and an average of $50,000 in fees and penalties. To help further protect you and your customers from the results of a data breach, Orbis Payment Solutions is now offering our merchants SecurityMetrics Assurance, which includes:

  • SecurityMetrics PANscan®, a data discovery tool
  • $100,000 breach coverage
  • A data protection policy
  • One hour of free consulting with a SecurityMetrics security expert
While you already receive protection under the $100,000 breach coverage, you can access additional SecurityMetrics Assurance resources by logging into your account at www.securitymetrics.com.

If you have any questions about PCI compliance or SecurityMetrics Assurance, please contact SecurityMetrics at 801.705.5700 or email support@securitymetrics.com.

Payment Gateways

Overview

Virtual terminals and payment gateways are an increasingly popular choice amongst small business owners, schools, and nonprofit organizations that want to process credit cards, but prefer not to purchase a physical terminal, or, mostly accept payments in a card not present (key-entered) environment. This card processing solution is easy to use, convenient, and available so long as an internet connection is established.

The Orbis Advantage

We provide online gateways for businesses processing payments online. This includes online stores, recurring payment systems, and mobile payment processing. We can integrate with all e-commerce shopping carts and online stores. Orbis can provide a better rate than our online competitors when acting as your third party merchant service provider. We offer online reporting tools, analytics, and the ability to process ACH transactions. As well as the following:

  • Process sales from Visa®, MasterCard®, Discover & American Express
  • Integrate with Quickbooks, Filemaker, accounting software
  • Integrate with Shopify, Magento, and other e-commerce platforms
  • View transaction history
  • View purchasing analytics
  • Set up scheduled recurring payments
  • Send email receipts to customers
  • Collect full customer details: name, address, phone, email, notes
  • Multiple devices can charge to the same merchant account
  • Secure transactions – PCI compliant

Terminals

Overview

Credit card terminals allow merchants to process sales with a credit card. They have the option to swipe, insert, or key enter the customers credit card. The credit card terminal then transmits this data to Orbis to process the transaction. Most new terminal models now process gift cards, checks, and the standard debit / credit cards. Orbis offers terminals with a large range of features, many of which are specific to the merchants industry. We can assist you in finding the terminal option that fits your business.

The Orbis Advantage

  • Acceptance of credit and debit cards
  • Key Entry (for Customer Not Present mail and telephone order)
  • Tips and Gratuities
  • Refunds and Adjustments
  • Pre-Authorization
  • Remote Initialization and Software update
  • POS Integration
  • Multi-merchant Capabilities
  • Pen or PIN authorization from the customer
  • Surcharge Function
  • Secure Password Operation
  • WiFi – Ethernet – Store and Forward
  • Tips and Gratuities
  • Touch Screen – Color Screen – Handheld
